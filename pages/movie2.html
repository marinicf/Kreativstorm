The Lord of the Rings: The Two Towers (2002)

Frodo and Sam, accompanied by Gollum, journey towards Mordor, while Aragorn, Legolas, and Gimli seek to rescue Merry and Pippin. They encounter Gandalf, now Gandalf the White, and together they help free King Théoden from Saruman's influence. Théoden prepares for battle against Saruman's forces at Helm's Deep, aided by Lothlorien Elves. Meanwhile, Merry and Pippin ally with Treebeard and the Ents, who attack Isengard, trapping Saruman. At Helm's Deep, the defenders face overwhelming odds but are saved by Gandalf and Éomer's arrival with the Rohirrim. Gollum leads Frodo and Sam towards Mordor but is captured by Rangers led by Faramir, who learns of the One Ring. Frodo convinces Faramir to release them, but Gollum's loyalty wavers as he plans to lead them into danger.
