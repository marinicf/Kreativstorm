The Lord of the Rings: The Fellowship of the Ring (2001)

In the Second Age of Middle-earth, Sauron, the Dark Lord, forges the One Ring to dominate the other Rings of Power. A final alliance of Men and Elves defeats Sauron, but Isildur, corrupted by the Ring, takes it for himself. The Ring is lost for centuries until it falls into the hands of Bilbo Baggins, who passes it to his nephew Frodo. Gandalf discovers the Ring's true nature and warns Frodo to flee. Accompanied by friends, Frodo sets out to destroy the Ring in Mount Doom. Along the way, they face dangers, including attacks from Sauron's forces and betrayal from Saruman.
